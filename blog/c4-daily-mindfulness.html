Daily Mindfulness

This is an interesting concept I've heard of before, when many people are mostly just waiting for their turn to speak, rather than actively listening. I found it easy as the listener, as did my flatmate. We hang out a lot so we have proper conversations like this, so it was pretty easy. The bigger challenge was speaking for a 3 minutes monologue, as I felt the need to talk about deep stuff, which actually caused me some mild anxiety. Probably not the point of the lesson but oh well.

I like 'do less than you can' - I definitely resonate with the idea that the novelty starts to wear off pretty quickly and it becomes hard to maintain. That and one breath a day seem like a good way of getting over that hump. Home
